Enter Sandman Tunnel Experience

The Enter Sandman Tunnel Experience continues to be one of our most popular gameday traditions for Monogram Club members. This past year, over 80 members participated in the event.

  • What it is: Members enjoy a Monogram Club tailgate followed by exclusive access to line the tunnel as the Hokies take the field.
  • When: The event takes place during a select Virginia Tech home football game, typically Homecoming weekend.
